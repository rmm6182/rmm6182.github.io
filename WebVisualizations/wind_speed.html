Wind Speed Visual

This visualization shows the comparison of Latitude to Wind Speed. Latitude is on the X-axis (bottom axis). Wind Speed, which is measured in miles per hour (mph), is on the Y-axis (left axis).

There is an very interesting relationship between Latitude and Wind Speed. Towards the Northern Hemisphere, there are several outliers. The outliers are above the average of 20 mph, and ranging into the 40's to high 50's mph.
